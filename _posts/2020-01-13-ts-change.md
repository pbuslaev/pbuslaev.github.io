To study the structural properties of lipids which influence the phase transition most we performed atomistic simulations of DPPC bilayer at temperatures 325, 320, 318 and 315 K. We compared the dynamical properties for the lipids along all possible principle components.

![](/img/ts_ratio_vs_pc_1.png)

The PC80 and PC93 are the most important ones since they are slowed down strongly. They include the rotation of some dihedrals. Thus, the next step would be to calculate the distributions of all the dihedrals and to compare them for different temperatures. That is done with the [developed script](https://github.com/pbuslaev/scr/blob/master/calc_dihed.py).
