The DPPC lipid in CHARMM36 representation is:

<pre>
    C14           O13
    |             |
C15-N-C12-C11-O12-P - O14 
    |             |
    C13      C1 - O11   O22
             |          |
             C2 - O21 - C21-C22-C23-C24-C25-C26-C27-C28-C29-C210-C211-C212-C213-C214-C215-C216
             |
             C3 - O31 - C31-C32-C33-C34-C35-C36-C37-C38-C39-C310-3211-C312-C313-C314-C315-C316
                        |
                        O32
</pre>

At temperatures 325, 320, 318 and 315 K distributions for all possible dihedrals were calculated. There is no significant difference there.
